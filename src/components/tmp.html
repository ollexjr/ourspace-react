About Us

A discreet search service

We offer a private client property search service with a business presence since 2018. Operating in the South East of England we solely represent individual buyers seeking high end property. Being based in the region we have an in-depth knowledge which we use together with your detailed brief to conduct a discreet focused search.

Saving you time and energy

We conduct initial viewings, provide you with property information and accompany you on viewings of the most suitable, desirable properties.

Additionally, giving you reassurance in acting as your buying agent as required. We are very excited about meeting you, discussing your requirements, detailing how we will help you, receiving your instruction and finding your new residence.

Our Fees

Our fees include an initial retainer fee followed by a success fee payable between exchange and completion and, if applicable, an oversee of purchase fee.

Whether you intend to purchase or rent, we invite you to contact us to discuss your personal circumstances and receive a detailed written breakdown of our fees based on your individual intended spend, by way of an example.

At Prestigious Address we do not hold client money.
